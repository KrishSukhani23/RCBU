Building picasso


A drawing competition like none other, Building
Picasso brings out the creativity and the artistic flair of over 900 children
of grades Jr. KG through 8 from schools all over Mumbai.

  • Date: 27th January 2019
  • Venue: RSC Wadala
  • Avenue: Club Service

I-Lit

We at RCBU give Literacy paramount importance
and to lead the crusade, we have I-Lit. A literature festival for children of
schools, NGOs, orphanages and backward communities where we spread the message
of literacy through literature and instil in young minds to take up literature
through unconventional ways.

  • Date: Year Round
  • Venue: N/A
  • Avenue: PD & PIS

HOPE 3.0

CANCER IS GOING TO BE ONLY A STORY, NOT YOUR WHOLE LIFE.
With our legacy project HOPE, which is a fight against Cancer. We, The Rotaract Club of Bombay Uptown supports the Fighters, admire the Survivors, honored the Taken, and never give up HOPE.

  • Date: Year Round
  • Venue: N/A

Project neev

Along with our Rotary Club, we host a 3 day fair
for amputees where each one is provided a personalised Jaipur foot or arm,
wheelchairs, crutches and more, absolutely free of cost! With beneficiaries
from children to elderly, from pan India, coming down to get a new hope of
life, this event changes the life of people, forever.

  • Date: April
  • Venu: RSC Wadala
  • Avenue: COM Serve, PIS

world disability day

To celebrate World Disability Day, we organise
a cultural fiesta for specially abled children and young adults. The day starts
with a rally and ends with fabulous performances by the specially abled,
showcasing different cultures.

  • Date: December
  • Venue: RSC Wadala
  • Avenue: IS, PIS

the touch

Focussing on women empowerment, we target young
girls across schools and slums to make them aware about the difference between
good touch and bad touch. Imparting this essential difference goes onto help
them a long way ahead in their lives.

  • Date: Year Round
  • Venue: Multiple
  • Avenue: COM Serve
